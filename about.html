About

Scientist, Engineer, Coder

I spent my life as an expat, living and traveling the world. At the end of 2020 I was able to fulfill one of my dreams and make aliyah. I have an undergraduate degree in chemistry and a graduate degree in theoretical physics. My career has allowed me to explore a variety of fields including petrochemical, engineering, startups, programming, and owning my own gym.

I am interested in coding because it is fun. I like to build things and solve problems.

Personal Interests

I enjoy working out, lifting heavy things, and walking on my hands. I constantly seek ways to push my self physically, intellectually, and creatively. I like to sew, paint, and tinker with interior design/decorating.

Cities of my Life

I was born in New Orleans, Louisiana and spent my early childhood in Norway and England. I went to university in Texas followed by graduate school in Colorado. My career took me through Arizona, California, New Jersey, and Georgia. Finally, I am home in Israel.
